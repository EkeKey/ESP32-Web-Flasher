EkeKey ESP32 Flasher

⚡ Important Instructions:
  1. Use a good data USB cable.
  2. Click Flash and select the port. Choose the COM port to unlock the Connect button.
  3. If the port does not appear or the board fails to connect:
    - Hold BOOT.
    - Press and release RESET while still holding BOOT.
    - Release BOOT.

Alternative: unplug USB, hold BOOT, plug USB back in, then release BOOT.
